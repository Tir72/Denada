About us

Hola!

De Nada is a London based Spanglish (half Spanish, half English) creative agency formed in 2013 by Tirso Otaegui and Benji Taylor. They’re passionate about making their clients shine and their audiences smile.

Benji Taylor

Co Founder and Head of Advertising

Benji Taylor is an award winning concepting copywriter with a wealth of experience in the worldof advertising.Having produced award winning and business winning work at agencies such asDare, DDB London, VCCP, Euro RSCG and many more, he also teaches Creative Advertising at a HE level at such schools as Central St Martins, Ravensbourne, The School of Communication Arts amongst Others. His projects have ranged from the digital to traditional media and have helped brands such as Vodafone, Durex, Sony, Irish Distillers and Talk to Frank.

Tirso S. Otaegui

Co Founder and Head of Art / Head of Design

Tirso S. Otaegui is a designer with excellent experience in many different fields of graphics. Working in Spain and London he has produced print work, web design, created brands and fitted out restaurants with imagery that’s gone down as well as the food. In Spain he spent his time in creative agencies such as Propias Publicidad, Nuria Carballo Publicidad and Eurorot, then moved to London and worked with London Graphics before setting up De Nada.
